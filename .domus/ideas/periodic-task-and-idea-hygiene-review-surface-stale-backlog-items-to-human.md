# Idea: Periodic task and idea hygiene review — surface stale backlog items to human

**Captured:** 2026-03-17
**Status:** raw

---

## The Idea

As the project evolves, outstanding tasks and ideas become irrelevant but stay in the backlog as noise. Need a periodic review mechanism (Doctor-type persona) that audits open tasks/ideas against current direction, surfaces inconsistencies and recommendations to the human, who decides: update, cancel, defer, or split. Core philosophy: LLM performance degrades with noisy context — clean, focused context is a first-class concern, not housekeeping.
