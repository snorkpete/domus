**Captured:** 2026-03-15

## The Idea

Design the full observability experience for domus: what does the human see when workers are running, stalled, or done? What's the right surface — task watch, a dedicated pipeline view, terminal output, something else? Inputs we already have: worker branch on task, execution logs, audit log, task status. The 'last 5 done in overview' was one narrow suggestion. The real question is: what does an operator need to feel in control of autonomous work happening on their behalf?
