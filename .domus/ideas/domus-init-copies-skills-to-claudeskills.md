# Idea: domus init copies skills to ~/.claude/skills/

**Captured:** 2026-03-15
**Status:** raw

---

## The Idea

When setup/skills/ exists in cwd (i.e. you're running init from the domus repo), domus init copies skill directories to ~/.claude/skills/, skipping existing. Makes the global machine setup a single command. Later: --force flag to overwrite, or promote to domus upgrade.
