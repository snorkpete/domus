# Idea: Human-in-the-loop pre-requisites as a first-class refinement concept

**Captured:** 2026-03-15
**Status:** raw

---

## The Idea

Some tasks can't reach autonomous without a human answering a question first (e.g. 'does the money field support X?'). This should be a first-class concept in the refinement workflow — not just an open question in the task body, but a tracked gate that blocks autonomous promotion until answered. Answering it naturally either closes the gate (task proceeds) or spawns a new blocking task. Domus should make this pattern explicit and easy to act on.
